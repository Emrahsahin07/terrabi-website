Product Description

Our Organic Basmati Rice is cultivated in the pristine valleys of Uzbekistan, where the perfect combination of climate, soil, and traditional farming practices creates rice of exceptional quality. Each grain is carefully grown, harvested, and processed to preserve its natural aroma, flavor, and nutritional properties.

Basmati rice is renowned for its distinctive fragrance, delicate flavor, and long, slender grains that cook up fluffy with each grain separate. Our organic basmati rice offers these classic characteristics while also providing the benefits of organic cultivation – no synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers are used in its production.

Key Features:

  • 100% certified organic
  • Extra-long grain variety (7.5mm+)
  • Distinctive natural aroma
  • Low glycemic index
  • Aged for optimal flavor development
  • Free from artificial additives and preservatives
  • Sustainably grown using traditional methods

Origin & Cultivation

Our basmati rice is grown in the fertile valleys of eastern Uzbekistan, where the unique combination of mineral-rich soil, pure mountain water, and traditional farming techniques creates the perfect conditions for premium rice cultivation. The fields are carefully managed by experienced farmers who have been growing rice for generations.

Processing & Quality Control

After harvesting, our rice undergoes a meticulous cleaning and aging process. Each batch is tested for purity, moisture content, and quality before packaging. Our state-of-the-art processing facility ensures that only the finest grains make it to your table.
