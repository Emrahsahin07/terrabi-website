Our Cultivation Methods

At Terrabi, we take pride in our sustainable and traditional farming practices that ensure the highest quality organic products.

Natural Fertilizers

We use only natural fertilizers such as compost, manure, and green manures to enrich our soil. This ensures that our products are free from synthetic chemicals while promoting soil health.

Crop Rotation

We implement systematic crop rotation to naturally maintain soil fertility, reduce pest pressure, and prevent soil-borne diseases, resulting in healthier plants and higher quality produce.

Water Conservation

Our drip irrigation systems and water management practices ensure efficient water usage while maintaining optimal growing conditions for our crops, preserving this precious resource.
